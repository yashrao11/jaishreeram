Ram Mandir is located in Ayodhya in Uttar Pradesh. Its groundbreaking had held on 5th August, 2020 and it will be inaugrated on 22nd January, 2024. This temple is located at the place where Lord Ahree Rama born or Ram Janam Bhoomi. According to Ramayana Shree Ram was born in Ayohya.
History

There was a big temple at this place. But it was attacked by Mughal emperor Babur in 16th century. Then the Mughals destroyed the temple and built a Masjid at that named Babri Masjid. Then for many years there was the Mosque. But in 1949 the people of Hindu Community found some clues about the temple of Lord Shree Ram like Murtis of Shri Ram, Sita etc.
This was the happening of 22-23 December 1949. The next day the devotees of Shree Rama began to gather there. But the Muslim devotees were protesting Hindu and Muslim people known as Ram Mandir Andolan. Then finally in 2020 the andolan conclued with the final decision to make Ram Mandir at the place of Mosque.
The Prime Minister of India Shree Narendra Modi perform the Ground Breaking Ceremony or Bhoomi Poojan on 5th August 2020.

Architecture

Temple is designed by Sompura family of Ahmedabad in 1988. The designer were Chandrakant Sompura and his two sons Nikhil Sompura and Ashish Sompura.

Size and Style

The temple is 380 feet long, 250 feet wide, 161 feet tall. It has 3 stories each of 20 feet tall with 392 pillars and 44 doors. It is build in Nagara Style.
Building Material

Temple is mad up of pink Sandstone and Indian traditional technology
Others The temple is built in only 3 acres of land and in remaining 97 acres, there are Mandapas, parking, water treatment plant etc.
The devotees climb 32 stairs and enter the temple by Singh Dwar and left the temple from the South direction. There are idols of Hanuman, Jatayu etc on the Singh Dwar
